Nowcast

Short-term extreme heat event prediction using ERA5 reanalysis and deep learning.

Forecasting dangerous heat conditions 1–7 days ahead for vulnerable urban populations, with physiological severity classification.

Pipeline

1

ERA5 Data

Hourly temperature and dewpoint for target city (20 years)

2

Decomposition

LOWESS smoothing to extract mean diurnal cycle + stochastic residuals

3

Autoencoder

Anomaly detection on residual patterns to flag unusual weather

4

GRU

Sequential prediction of extreme event probability from 100-hour lookback

5

EHI-N*

Physiological severity — zone classification by metabolic rate and sun
