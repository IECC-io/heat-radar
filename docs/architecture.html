Stochastic Variability Decomposition

LOWESS smoothing extracts the mean diurnal cycle for each variable. Residuals capture day-to-day weather variability — the signal that matters for extreme event detection.

3 Physics Model No Training

EHI-N* Zone Classification

Analytical heat balance equations. Maps (T, RH, MET, sun) → physiological zone. This is the ground truth for "what matters."

Zone 1–3: Safe  |  Zone 4: Sweating  |  Zone 5–6: Danger
