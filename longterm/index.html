Longterm

Future heat stress projections through 2100 using CMIP6 climate models and machine learning.

Projecting how physiological heat stress will evolve under different emission scenarios, combining ensemble climate data with the EHI-N* model.

Pipeline

1

CMIP6 Data

NASA NEX-GDDP bias-corrected projections at 0.25° resolution

2

Preprocessing

Regional extraction, unit conversion, feature engineering

3

EHI-N*

Physiological heat index calculation with variable MET and solar

4

ML Ensemble

Random Forest + XGBoost + stacked meta-learner

5

Projections

Gridded future heat stress maps through 2100

SSP Scenarios

SSP1-2.6

Sustainability pathway. Strong mitigation, warming peaks below 2°C. Best-case scenario for heat stress.

SSP2-4.5

Middle of the road. Moderate mitigation, warming reaches ~2.7°C by 2100. Current trajectory baseline.

SSP5-8.5

Fossil-fueled development. Minimal mitigation, warming exceeds 4°C. Worst-case heat stress projection.
